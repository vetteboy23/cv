By that time, I had become frustrated with the healthcare industry and how
difficult it is to operate a clinic profitably while serving patients how
I want to.

As a result, I left clinical practice. Since that time, my focus has been
on discovering, developing, and teaching solutions that people can do in
the comfort of their home to take charge of their health and hopefully
decrease their need for in-clinic healthcare services.
